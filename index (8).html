What BrainyAct gives you

The system pays for care. But rarely knows if it worked.

For most high-cost medical categories, there is a protocol, a target, and an endpoint. Surgery has discharge criteria. Cardiac rehab has a program window. Physical therapy has functional goals. Autism and ADHD care often do not. That leaves every stakeholder stuck in the same cycle:

🏥
Health Plans
Rising behavioral health utilization, long waitlists, authorization pressure, and limited outcome visibility.
🏢
Self-Insured Employers
Open-ended claims exposure, caregiver productivity loss, and no clear way to measure benefit value.
🔄
TPAs
Renewal conversations where behavioral health trend keeps growing without a defensible outcome story.
🤝
Brokers & Consultants
Client demand for new cost-containment strategies that do not look like benefit cuts.

Nobody is gaming the system. The system just has no exit.

BrainyAct creates a measurable path forward.
